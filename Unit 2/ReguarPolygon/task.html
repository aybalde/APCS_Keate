Regular Polygon

A regular polygon is a convex polygon that has equal side lengths and angle measurements. These shapes have formulas to find their interior angle and exterior angle measurements and area based on their side length and number of sides. You can read more about regular polygons here The formulas are as follows (assume s represents the side length and n represents the number of sides):

interior angle measurement = (n-2)*180/n, measurement is in degrees
exterior angle measurement = 360/n, measurement is in degrees
area = (s2*n)/(4tan(Pi/n))
